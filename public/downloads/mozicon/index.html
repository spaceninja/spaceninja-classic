Alternate Mozilla Icon

The early builds and pre-version-one releases of Mozilla included a really ugly icon of a blue gecko. I created this icon from the mozilla logo on their homepage in November of 2001 and offered it for download here. Eventually, the mozilla team got a new icon, and then later Firefox took over and had an even better icon, so this isn't really needed anymore, except for historical purposes.
- Scott Vandehey
