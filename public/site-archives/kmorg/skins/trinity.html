What's this?
Trinity is a game invented by Miles Johnson. The rules are simple. Given three movies, picked randomly, each player tries to come up with the best movie idea that synthesizes all three of the given movies. For example, given Raiders of the Lost Ark, Reservoir Dogs and The Crow, here are some possiblities:

  • "But Sometimes..." The team from Reservoir Dogs plan and execute a heist on the secret warehouse where the Ark of the Covenant is stored. After successfully making off with the Ark, the theives kill Indiana Jones, who has infiltrated the group. A crow summons Indy's spirit back from the afterlife, and he bursts from his unmarked grave as a whip-wielding vigilante in black. In a climatic battle atop a church, he massacres the heartless felons, and at last his soul can rest.
  • "Reservoir Crows" A team of archeologists returns from the dead to set things right after being brutally murdered by Nazis in a ferocious gun battle for the Ark of the Covenant. While trying to recover the holy relic from the goose-steppers, the team must fight betrayal from within as they realize that one of them is a Nazi Informer... But which one?
  • "Indiana Jones and the Wrath of der Fuhrer" Indiana Jones infiltrates the Nazis when he hears of their plan to steal the Ark. Once among their ranks, he finds himself teamed with an old flame from before the War, who uses him to find the Ark, and then kills him. A crow brings his soul back as a spirit of vengeance, and he must travel across the now Nazi-controlled Europe to find the Ark, destroy it, and take down the Fuhrer. But Hitler is dead and no one has ever laid eyes on his successor...
