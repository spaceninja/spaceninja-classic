Please note

    Use this form to request the installation of additional software on a classroom / lab computer or server. In order to get your software installed on time we must have your software request submitted and the software media and license(s) in our office at least two weeks prior to the class' commencement. Please deliver the original media and licenses to SH134. The media and the licenses will remain in our office as long as the software is installed on the system(s). At the end of your class please make arrangements to pick up the software. Software not retrieved will be archived for three years. Please note that software requests can last for up to one full year (i.e. From fall 2000 through summer 2001) before they need to be resubmitted using this form. For questions call 725-3226
