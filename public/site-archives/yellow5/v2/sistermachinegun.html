Ah... Christopher Randall of Sister Machine Gun! Sorry about the prior email, it took a visit to the positron records page before I connected your name with your band.

I have only the highest respect for you and your music. So please allow me to explain what's happened.

Yellow Number Five doesn't actually exist. I'm a graphic designer, and in high school I made up a fictional band so I could do CD cover designs. I named the band after a small joke between some friends and I, and used song titles from my favorite music to fill the album. Later, a friend and I got really excited about the whole idea, and bought the domain yellow5.com, and created an entire fake history for the band. We used photos of ourselves and made up fake names and personalities. We "created" four albums for the band, each one filled with supposed covers of songs that we enjoyed. We included a cover of a Sister Machine Gun song on the second album (Gas Food Lodging).

As you can see, no actual crime has taken place, and I apologize if you were misled. We leave the site up because it amuses us to document the fake history of this band. Take a peek at the news stories sometime... they're all pretty odd stuff, but no matter how odd, people still seem to believe it. We get fan mail. We've never advertised, but people find the band and tell us that they love our music, which doesn't exist!

My apologies once again on the misunderstanding, and (artist to artist) I hope you understand that no disrespect was meant towards your music.
